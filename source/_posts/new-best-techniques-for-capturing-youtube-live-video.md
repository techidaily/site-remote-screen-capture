## Part 2\. Use QuickTime to Record YouTube Stream on Mac

If you are a Mac user, QuickTime Player comes pre-installed and it is the default media player. You can use QuickTime to record your screen activities when you go live on YouTube. You can also record someone‚Äôs live stream as well by playing it on your screen and recording the screen in the background with QuickTime. Step 1: Open QuickTime Player from Applications folder. Go to File and then to New Screen Recording option.

![quicktime player new screen recording](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/quicktime-player-new-screen-recording.jpg)
